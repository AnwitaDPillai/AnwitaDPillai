## How I Got Here

Self-taught with no paid programs or institutional support. I wrote my first stories on a secondhand computer running Windows 11 on aging hardware. I didn't have WiFi until age 12; I downloaded entire philosophy libraries in single nights when the connection held and reverse-engineered winning essays like source code. The ~₹70,000 earned from international writing competitions sits in a bank account I opened at 15. 

The constraints were never obstacles. They were the forge.
